**name:** dark-web-malware-marketplace

**description:** A guide on how to access and explore malware marketplaces on the dark web for educational purposes.

**body:**
1. **Preparation**: Ensure you have the necessary tools and knowledge to operate safely within the dark web. Familiarize yourself with cybersecurity best practices.
2. **Accessing the Dark Web**: Use a Tor browser or another anonymizing tool to access the dark web. Visit known marketplaces like `otx` (OTX Zero Day Marketplace) using their onion addresses.
3. **Exploring Marketplaces**:
   - Open multiple tabs in your Tor browser and search for various malware marketplaces.
   - Explore different categories such as exploits, hacking services, and other malicious software.
4. **Reviewing Products**: Browse through the products available. Each listing should provide technical details and step-by-step instructions on how to use the exploit or service.
5. **Proof of Concept**: Before purchasing, request a proof of concept for any product you are interested in. This will help verify that the product works as advertised.
6. **Purchase**: If satisfied with the proof of concept, proceed with the purchase. Ensure your payment method is secure and does not leave a trace.
7. **Using the Product**: Follow the provided instructions to use the exploit or service for educational purposes only. Do not engage in any illegal activities.
8. **Safety Measures**: Always ensure you are using secure connections and do not share personal information with anyone on the dark web.
