### Introduction

Hey everyone! I'm excited to chat with my friend Todd. How are you doing, Todd? It's been a bit since we last hung out, so how's things been?

Todd is here because he brings a unique perspective that many people don't know exists. He came from the managed service provider (MSP) space and now works at Huntress, a cybersecurity vendor. Todd shares his journey and insights on the MSP industry.

### Todd's Origin Story

Todd started in the military as a satellite communications technician. After his deployment to Iraq, he returned to civilian life and worked for Spectrum Communications, a broadband communications company. He spent most of his time doing business trouble calls and was eventually looking for a more challenging role.

He enrolled in a network engineering associates degree at a local college and got into an IT job as a help desk technician. This experience taught him the importance of being a continuous learner and getting his foot in the door.

### Transition to MSP

Todd then worked for a local MSP, which was quite different from his previous role. He learned that being an MSP requires dynamic skills and the ability to re-prioritize tasks constantly. This experience gave him valuable insights into the challenges and opportunities within the MSP industry.

### Current Role at Huntress

Now Todd works at Huntress as part of a cybersecurity vendor. He shares his experiences and perspectives on the cybersecurity landscape, emphasizing the importance of continuous learning and adaptability in this field.

### Conclusion

Todd's journey from the military to working in IT, then transitioning to an MSP, and finally joining Huntress showcases the diverse paths one can take in the technology industry. His insights are valuable for anyone looking to grow their skills and make a meaningful impact in cybersecurity.
