  ## Introduction to CPTS Certification

  Hello everyone! I'm Dimitris, the Training Director at Hack the Box Academy. Today, I want to discuss our newly launched Certified Penetration Testing Specialist (CPTS) certification and how it can benefit you in your cybersecurity career.

  ### What is CPTS?

  The CPTS certification is designed to provide a comprehensive understanding of penetration testing and its role in cybersecurity. It covers everything from beginner-level skills to advanced techniques, ensuring that professionals are well-equipped to handle real-world threats.

  ### Why Choose CPTS?

  There are several credible training providers in the cybersecurity market, each offering their own certifications. However, what sets CPTS apart is its focus on practical, hands-on learning. Unlike other certifications, CPTS ensures that you can immediately deploy your skills in a professional environment with appropriate supervision.

  ### How Does CPTS Fit into the Market?

  The CPTS certification is equivalent to well-known certifications like OSCP and ECPTP. It provides a pathway from beginner to intermediate penetration testing, making it an ideal choice for professionals looking to advance their careers in cybersecurity.

  ### Benefits of CPTS

  - **Practical Learning**: Emphasis on hands-on experience to ensure you can apply your skills effectively.
  - **Deployable Skills**: Ready-to-use skills that can be immediately deployed by employers with appropriate supervision.
  - **Comprehensive Coverage**: Covers all aspects of penetration testing, from basic techniques to advanced strategies.

  ### Conclusion

  The CPTS certification is a valuable resource for anyone looking to enhance their cybersecurity skills. By providing practical, hands-on learning and ensuring deployable skills, it sets you up for success in the rapidly evolving field of cybersecurity.
