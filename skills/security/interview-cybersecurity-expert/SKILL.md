name: interview-cybersecurity-expert
description: A step-by-step guide to interviewing a cybersecurity expert about their journey and insights.
body: 
  - Step 1: Start the interview with a friendly greeting and express excitement about having the expert join.
  - Step 2: Ask the expert about their background and how they got into the field of cybersecurity, prompting them to share their personal journey.
  - Step 3: Encourage them to discuss their experiences with different roles and projects they have been involved in throughout their career.
  - Step 4: Dive into specific skills they find essential in cybersecurity and how they have developed these skills over time.
  - Step 5: Inquire about any challenges they've faced in the industry and how they've overcome them, allowing insights into resilience and problem-solving.
  - Step 6: Discuss current trends in cybersecurity and gather their thoughts on emerging threats and technologies.
  - Step 7: Wrap up the discussion by asking for advice they'd give to someone starting in cybersecurity, summarizing key takeaways for aspiring professionals.
