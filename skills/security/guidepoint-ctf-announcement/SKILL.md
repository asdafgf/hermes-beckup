  In this video, it is announced that GuidePoint Security is hosting another Capture The Flag (CTF) competition aimed at intermediate players. The event will take place on Tuesday, June 22nd at 8 AM Eastern Time and will last until Monday, June 28th at 5 PM Eastern Time.

  The CTF will involve mid-level challenges that include tasks such as:
  - Decrypting codes using brute force techniques
  - Discovering and exploiting vulnerabilities
  - Gaining access to network devices
  - Becoming the super user or root admin

  Whether participants are experienced players or newcomers, this CTF is designed for learning and enjoyment. The first person to complete the CTF with the fastest time will win a $100 gift card. 

  Participation is free and can be done entirely online. Interested players can register by following a link provided in the video description. Upon registration, participants will receive an email granting them VPN access and additional information needed for entering the environment when the event begins.

  This is a great opportunity for players to solve challenges, learn more about cybersecurity, and have fun in a competitive setting. All players are encouraged to participate for a chance to showcase their skills and potentially win prizes.
