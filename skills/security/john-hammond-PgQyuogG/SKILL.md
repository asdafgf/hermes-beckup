# Hermes Agent

## Description
The Hermes Agent is a powerful tool designed to assist with security operations, including penetration testing and threat detection. This guide provides insights into its functionalities and how to effectively utilize it for various security challenges.

## Guide
### Overview
The Hermes Agent is part of a broader suite of security tools that afford security professionals the opportunity to enhance their skills through practical engagements. Participating in competitions like Capture The Flag (CTF) events, such as the Cyber Apocalypse CTF, can significantly improve your proficiency.

### Functions
- **Penetration Testing**: Use Hermes to simulate attacks and identify vulnerabilities in systems.
- **Threat Detection**: Monitor activities to detect potential breaches in real-time.
- **Education and Training**: Utilize resources and challenges, such as those provided by Hack The Box, to deepen your understanding of cybersecurity.

### Getting Started
1. **Registration**: To use Hermes, you should first register for events or platforms that support it, such as Hack The Box's CTF events.
2. **Team Formation**: Join or create a team of up to 10 members to participate effectively in competitions and share knowledge and skills.
3. **Resources**: Explore challenges available through Hack The Box, particularly those that range from beginner to intermediate levels.

### Participating in CTF
- **Event Details**: Keep an eye out for announcements regarding upcoming CTF competitions. For example, the Cyber Apocalypse CTF runs for five days, featuring various security challenges that cater to different skill levels.
- **Prizes and Donations**: Engage in these events not just for the challenge, but also to contribute to educational initiatives. Hack The Box donates to organizations like code.org based on participant engagement and challenge completions.

### Challenges and Prizes
- **Types of Challenges**: Expect a variety of categories including hardware and blockchain security, enhancing your skill set with unique problem-solving scenarios.
- **Rewards**: Participants can earn valuable prizes, including monetary rewards and educational resources (e.g., Hack The Box swag, training units).

### Conclusion
The Hermes Agent, through its integration with platforms like Hack The Box, serves as a critical resource for cybersecurity enthusiasts. Embrace its capabilities to sharpen your skills and contribute to the community through active participation in CTF events.
