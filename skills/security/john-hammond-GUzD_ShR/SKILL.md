**name:** Extract and Analyze Cyber Attack Data

**description:** Learn how to extract and analyze data from a cyber attack for forensic analysis.

**body:**
1. **Identify the Target System**: Determine the system that was attacked, such as servers of a psychotherapy center.
2. **Extract Data**: Use appropriate tools like `foremost`, `dd`, or `volatility` to extract data from the compromised system.
3. **Analyze Extracted Data**:
   - **Data Integrity Check**: Ensure the integrity and authenticity of the extracted data using checksums and hashes.
   - **Search for Sensitive Information**: Look for personal information such as phone numbers, email addresses, real addresses, social security numbers, and therapy notes.
4. **Review Therapy Notes**: Analyze the stolen therapy notes to understand the depth and sensitivity of the data compromised.
5. **Report Findings**: Compile a detailed report outlining the nature of the attack, the data extracted, and any potential risks associated with the theft.
