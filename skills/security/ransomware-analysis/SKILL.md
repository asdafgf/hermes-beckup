name: ransomware-analysis
description: Analysis and insights from a recent ransomware case.
body: |
  This skill provides a detailed guide on understanding the typical structure and implications of a ransomware attack. It includes deconstruction of a ransomware ransom note as part of an incident response investigation. The ransom note is representative of common trends in ransomware communication, highlighting tactics used by attackers to instill fear and urgency. Key components discussed include:

  - Personalization of the ransom note (inclusion of victim IDs)
  - Reassurances regarding data encryption methods (RSA and AES)
  - Threat of data destruction and exposure if the ransom is not paid
  - Offers of proof (decrypting a small number of files for free)
  - Communication methods for the attackers (and recommended use of encrypted email)
  - Possible payment methods and urgency in payment timelines
  - Structure and persistence mechanisms used by the ransomware malware

  The skill aims to educate users in recognizing the signs of a ransomware attack, the psychological strategies employed by attackers, and the importance of having a response plan in place.
