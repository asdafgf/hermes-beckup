### Setting Up Open Claw

1. **Get a VPS**
   - Visit [Hostinger](https://hostinger.com/ncopenclaw) for a one-click setup.
   - Choose KVM 2 and use the coupon code `NetworkChuck` for a discount.

2. **SSH into Your Server**
   - Once your server is ready, copy the SSH command and paste it into your terminal to log in.

3. **Install Open Claw**
   - Follow the documentation provided by the Open Claw team to install the gateway on your VPS.
