  ## Overview
  Guidepoint Security is hosting a Capture the Flag (CTF) competition designed for intermediate players on **June 22nd, 8 a.m. Eastern Time**. This challenge is a great opportunity to enhance your cybersecurity skills, learn, and have fun.

  ## Event Details
  - **Event Date**: June 22nd - June 28th (8 a.m. to 5 p.m. Eastern Time)
  - **Skill Level**: Intermediate
  - **Competition Focus**:
    - Decrypting codes
    - Discovering and exploiting vulnerabilities
    - Gaining access to network devices
    - Achieving super user/root admin privileges

  ## Registration
  The event is entirely free and conducted online. To register:
  1. Click the registration link in the video description or displayed during the video.
  2. Fill in your name and relevant information.
  3. You will receive a confirmation email with VPN access and necessary information to participate.

  ## How to Prepare
  - Brush up on your skills in code decryption, vulnerability analysis, and network exploitation.
  - Review basic PowerShell commands and potential malware detection techniques.
  - Familiarize yourself with capture the flag rules and regulations to ensure a smooth experience.

  ## Prizes
  - The first participant to complete the CTF with the fastest time will win a $100 gift card. 

  ## Conclusion
  Join this event to challenge yourself, learn new skills, and possibly win a prize! Have fun, and good luck on the scoreboard!
