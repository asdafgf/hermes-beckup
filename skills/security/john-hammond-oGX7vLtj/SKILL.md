# hermes-agent

## Description
The Hermes Agent is a security-focused learning tool designed to guide users through the TryHackMe Advent of Cyber 2021 activities. This exercise aims to enhance users' skills in cybersecurity through a fun and engaging storyline involving Santa Claus and his missing laptop.

## Body
### Guide to Hermes Agent

Welcome to the Hermes Agent guide! If you're following along with the TryHackMe Advent of Cyber 2021, you’re in for a treat with Day 8’s task: “Santa’s Bag of Toys.” 

In this activity, you will embark on a journey to recover Santa's laptop, which has mysteriously gone missing. The suspected culprit? A minion from the Grinch Enterprise. Your mission is to determine which actor compromised Santa’s laptop and to recover his bag of toys.

#### Getting Started
1. **Access TryHackMe**: Navigate to [TryHackMe.com](https://tryhackme.com) and scroll down to find the "Join for Free" button to get started.
   
2. **Find Day 8**: Once you’re in, locate Day 8 of the Advent of Cyber. 

3. **Prepare Your Environment**: Click the "Start Machine" button for this specific task. This will open a virtual environment on the right-hand side of the screen where you can execute your exercises without needing to download additional tools.

4. **Understanding the Virtual Machine**: Note that this is not an attack box; it is set up for analysis. You won’t be using this machine to launch attacks against other systems but to investigate logs and recover the lost laptop.

5. **Observe the Tools**: You will work with PowerShell transcription logs recovered from Santa's laptop. These logs will give you insights into what happened prior to the theft, helping you identify the attacker.

#### Analyze and Learn
- Dive into the PowerShell logs for clues.
- Discuss your findings and any difficulties in the comments section for constructive feedback.
- Enjoy the learning process and provide suggestions on improving the exercise.

### Conclusion
Your feedback is invaluable, so please let us know your thoughts or any constructive criticism you might have. It's crucial to foster a positive learning environment. Have fun, and let’s get started on uncovering the mystery of Santa's missing laptop!
