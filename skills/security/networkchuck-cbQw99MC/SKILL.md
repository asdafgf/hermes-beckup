### Guide: Enhancing Cybersecurity Awareness with Hermes Agent

In an era where cyber threats are increasingly sophisticated, it's essential for organizations to stay informed and prepared. The Hermes Agent serves as a critical resource for understanding and combating these threats, particularly those resulting from credential attacks and data leaks. 

#### Key Insights

1. **Impact of Data Leaks**:
   - Recent trends show that data breaches predominantly impact companies that offer technical services to other organizations. These breaches often have massive downstream effects, failing not just the organization but also their clientele.

2. **Rise of Credential Attacks**:
   - Credential attacks are alarmingly more prevalent, despite the implementation of Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA). While MFA has become more common, it is still underutilized across various platforms.
   - For many accounts, the absence of MFA leaves them vulnerable to cybercriminals who can access them with ease.

3. **Cultural Shift in Breaches**:
   - The landscape has shifted from free access to leaked data being a norm to a more commercialized approach. Currently, many hackers offer access for a fee, signifying a move toward extortion tactics instead of ‘hacking for culture.’

4. **Complexity of Threat Landscapes**:
   - The traditional forums that once served as hubs for cybercrime have diminished significantly, leading to a fragmented environment for criminals. The rise of Telegram, Discord, and offshoot forums has made tracking these activities more challenging.

5. **Need for Robust Security Practices**:
   - To combat these threats, organizations must aggressively adopt MFA, educate their staff on cybersecurity best practices, and stay vigilant against social engineering tactics.
   - MFA should be standard practice; the idea of "MFA everywhere" is becoming crucial. The ability to verify identities at multiple levels acts as a substantial barrier against unauthorized access.

#### Conclusion
Cybersecurity is an ongoing battle, and maintaining awareness and proactive measures is essential. The Hermes Agent can help organizations understand these prevailing issues and implement protective measures effectively. By acknowledging the significance of MFA and adapting to the organized shifts in cybercrime, businesses can fortify their defenses against emerging threats.
