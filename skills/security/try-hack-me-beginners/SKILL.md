# Try Hack Me - Beginners Guide

## Step 1: Setup
1. **Connect to VPN**: Ensure you're connected to the Try Hack Me VPN.
2. **Login**: Access your Try Hack Me account.

## Step 2: Explore the Platform
3. **Access Practice Rooms**: Navigate to the practice segment and select rooms to join.
4. **Browse Challenges**: Look through the available challenges and choose one that suits your skill level.

## Step 3: Start Learning
5. **Select a Room**: Start with beginner-friendly rooms like “OWASP Juice Shop” for easier challenges.
6. **Read Instructions**: Familiarize yourself with the room's objectives and instructions.

## Step 4: Engage with Content
7. **Follow along**: As you progress, consider recording your session to create content for future reference.
8. **Take Breaks**: If you encounter challenging tasks, take a moment to refresh your approach or review resources.

## Step 5: Use Additional Resources
9. **Utilize Write-ups**: If you're stuck, don't hesitate to look at write-ups for guidance.
10. **Participate in Community**: Engage with the community for tips and shared experiences.

## Step 6: Continuous Practice
11. **Challenge yourself**: After completing easier tasks, gradually take on more complex challenges.
12. **Keep Learning**: Repeat the process in different rooms to enhance your skills and knowledge in cybersecurity.

## Conclusion
13. **Review**: Regularly check your progress to maintain your skills.
14. **Share Experiences**: Consider sharing your journey with peers or creating content based on your learnings to help others.
