# Step-by-Step Guide to Participate in the GuidePoint Capture The Flag Competition

1. **Event Announcement**: 
   - GuidePoint Security is hosting a Capture The Flag (CTF) competition specifically designed for intermediate players. 

2. **Event Details**:
   - **Date**: The competition starts on Tuesday, June 22nd at 8:00 AM Eastern Time and ends on Monday, June 28th at 5:00 PM Eastern Time.
   - **Type**: Online competition, free to participate.

3. **Registration**:
   - Visit the registration link provided in the video description.
   - Fill out the necessary information including your name and email.
   - Submit the registration form.

4. **Receive Confirmation**:
   - Once registered, you will receive a confirmation email containing:
     - VPN access details.
     - Instructions to enter the competition environment.

5. **Prepare for the Challenge**:
   - Review the types of challenges you will be facing, which may include:
     - Decrypting codes.
     - Brute-forcing passwords.
     - Discovering and exploiting vulnerabilities.
     - Gaining access to network devices.
     - Achieving super user/root access.

6. **Competition Day Preparation**:
   - On the day of the competition, ensure you have:
     - Your VPN connection set up and running.
     - All necessary tools ready for use.
   - Familiarize yourself with the competition guidelines and structure.

7. **Participate in the Competition**:
   - Log in to the competition environment at the designated start time.
   - Start tackling the challenges at your own pace.
   - Keep track of your points and time, aiming to solve as many challenges as possible.

8. **Post-Competition**:
   - After the competition concludes on June 28th at 5:00 PM, winners will be announced based on who completed the challenges in the fastest time and scored the most points.

9. **Enjoy the Learning Experience**:
   - Focus on having fun and learning throughout the competition, regardless of your final score or rank.
   
10. **Look Forward to Future Events**:
   - Keep an eye out for future CTF events hosted by GuidePoint Security for continued learning and skill improvement in cybersecurity.
